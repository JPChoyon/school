Welcome to Jashore Education Board

The Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE), Jashore,Bangladesh was established for Khulna division in 1963 by an Ordinance (vide East Pakistan Ordinance No. XXXIII of 1961) for the organization, regulation, supervision, control and development of Intermediate and Secondary level public examinations and educational institutions. The Ordinance was, however, amended in 1962 (No. XVI of 1962) and in 1977 (No. XVII of 1977). Dr. Abdul Haque was the founding chairman (9.10.1963 to 04.12.1965) of BISE, Jashore. Prof. Dr. Molla Amir Hossen is the present Chairman (29 January 2020 upto present) and 32th Chairman in the history of BISE, Jashore
